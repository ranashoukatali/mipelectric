About us

Company Profile

MIP Technologies a company which has been established with the aim of manufacturing those items which are not manufactured in Pakistan or if manufactured they are of cheap quality. MIP is committed to provide customers with the best quality products in lowest prices in the market. We are focusing on R&D to produce technologies domestically which are very expensive if they are imported like Injection Molding Machine, VMC , CNC ,PLC and custom ordered machines fully automatic and functional. .

Vision

MIP Technologies came into existence with the aim to overcome the current trade deficit issue with the high-quality products manufactured locally. MIP is not only committed to fulfilling the needs of Pakistan but also committed to exporting the products to foreign countries with a highly efficient and cost-effective method of production, which can overall help the end customers with competitive prices and best quality.

As well as ensure sustainable leadership position in Pakistan & increase global footprint in the electrical sector. Identify & capitalize on diversification opportunities to maximize production while remaining socially responsive in all spheres of operations.
